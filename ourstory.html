Our purpose

Rethread Studio was created to make thrifting easier, more aesthetic, and more accessible for young adults who love Y2K fashion.

How We Started

In 2025, we recognised that online thrifting can often feel overwhelming due to cluttered platforms and poor curation. This inspired us to establish Rethread Studio — a platform that offers a curated and accessible shopping experience for young adults.
